EMPTY STATE: Un-feature all products (or point at a state with none in stock) and reload `/en` — confirm the ENTIRE Featured Pieces band (eyebrow + heading + grid + "View All" button) is absent, with no empty container or placeholder, and the page below it (newsletter, footer) renders normally with no console errors.
